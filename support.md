# Nudgy Customer Support

Welcome to the Treest support page. We're here to help you get the most out of your English writing practice.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions, issues, or feedback, please reach out to our support team.

- **Email**: [support@treestcorp.com](mailto:support@treestcorp.com)
- **Response Time**: We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?

Please make sure you have a stable internet connection and that you're using the latest version of the app. If the issue
persists, please contact us using the email above.

### How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion, please contact us from the email address associated with your account. We will process your
request within 7 business days.

### Can I use Nudgy on multiple devices?

Your progress syncs across devices as long as you are signed in with the same account.

## Report a Bug

If you encounter a bug or unexpected behavior, please contact us with the following details:

- Device model and OS version
- App version
- A brief description of the issue
- Screenshots, if possible
